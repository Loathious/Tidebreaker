# ⚔️ Journey of Adventures

> *"The monsters have kidnapped the villagers. Please save them."*
> — A villager, probably crying

A 2D pixel-art action-adventure. Fight your way from a sleepy village through dark caves, a cursed jungle temple, a scorching desert pyramid, and onto the storming open ocean — all to punch a giant squid in the heart.

---

## 📖 The Story

It was supposed to be a normal day in the village.

Then the screaming started.

Monsters poured in from the forest. Villagers were dragged away. Nobody knew why — only that something ancient and deeply *evil* was pulling the strings from somewhere far away.

A weeping villager hands you a rusty old sword and begs for help. You step outside.

Five levels stand between you and the truth. At the end of it all, beneath a storming sky and crashing waves, the **Kraken** waits.

---

## 🗺️ The Journey

### 🏘️ Level 1 — The Village
*Monsters don't usually attack in broad daylight. Today they did.*

Zombies are everywhere. Three houses broken into. Three villagers to save. Your rusty sword has exactly 20 swings before it shatters — use them wisely.

After clearing the village, a grateful survivor hands you a **map** and points you toward the cave behind the village. There are diamonds down there. You're going to need a better sword.

---

### 🕳️ Level 2 — The Dark Cave
*You can barely see three feet ahead. Something just landed on the ceiling above you.*

Mine five diamonds from the rocks. Avoid the spiders dropping from above. Find the **Crafting Shrine** hidden in the dark and forge your new weapon.

The moment the Diamond Sword appears in your hands, the ground shakes.

A voice echoes through the cave:

> *"You are getting closer…"*

---

### 🌿 Level 3 — The Jungle Temple
*Ancient ruins swallowed by vines. Monkeys in the canopy. Something much bigger guards the door.*

Hack through vine snakes and dodge coconuts hurled by agitated monkeys as you push deeper toward the temple. The **Jungle Guardian** — a massive two-phase mini-boss — blocks the entrance.

Inside, carved into the stone wall:

> *"The source of darkness lies beyond the endless desert.*
> *Only the power of the ancient pyramid can weaken the ocean beast."*

---

### 🏜️ Level 4 — The Desert Pyramid
*The sun is blinding. The sand hides things that bite.*

Three ancient **Obelisks** must be activated before the pyramid opens its doors:

1. ☠️ Defeat the great **Sandworm** lurking beneath the dunes
2. 🧩 Cross the **moving platform puzzle** — no safety net
3. 🌊 Survive the **sandworm wave** — six of them, all at once

Inside the pyramid you find what you came for: **Magical Armor** and a **Bow**. You're going to need both of them.

> *"The armor will protect you from the sea beast. Now — sail to the Ocean."*

---

### 🌊 Level 5 — The Ocean
*Storm clouds. Lightning. Platforms crumbling into the sea. And rising from the deep…*

The **Kraken** — responsible for everything — has three phases of fury:

- **Phase 1 (100–70% HP)** — Three tentacles slam down onto the platforms. Shoot them with the bow.
- **Phase 2 (70–30% HP)** — Water waves, energy blasts, falling platforms. The armor absorbs one hit every 10 seconds. Pray.
- **Phase 3 (30–0% HP)** — The Kraken tears itself open. Its heart is exposed. Jump, dodge tentacles, and hit it ten times.

The beast has something to say before the fight begins:

> *"Who are you? You can't defeat me, I'm too powerful."*

Prove it wrong.

## ⚔️ Your Arsenal

| Item | Where you get it | What it does |
|---|---|---|
| 🗡️ **Rusty Sword** | Starting weapon | 15 damage — shatters after 20 hits |
| 💎 **Diamond Sword** | Crafted in the cave | 35 damage, completely unbreakable |
| 🏹 **Bow & Arrow** | Desert pyramid | Ranged — arrows fly toward your cursor |
| 🛡️ **Magical Armor** | Desert pyramid | Absorbs one hit every 10 seconds |

---

## 👾 Know Your Enemies

| | Enemy | Their deal |
|---|---|---|
| 🧟 | **Zombie** | Slow, relentless, climbs walls |
| 🕷️ | **Spider** | Drops from the ceiling when you least expect it |
| 🐍 | **Vine Snake** | Extremely fast. Lunges. Genuinely rude. |
| 🐒 | **Monkey** | Keeps its distance and throws things at you |
| 🦍 | **Jungle Guardian** | Mini-boss. Gets angrier past 50% HP. |
| 🪱 | **Sandworm** | Bursts from the sand. There's more than one. |
| 🦑 | **Kraken** | *The one responsible for all of this.* 1000 HP. |

## 🎮 Controls

| Action | Input |
|---|---|
| Move | `A` / `D` |
| Jump | `Space` |
| Dash | `Ctrl` + direction |
| Melee attack | `Left Click` |
| Shoot arrow | `Right Click` *(after the desert)* |
| Interact / craft | `E` |

> 💡 **Tip:** You can jump for a brief moment after walking off a ledge. Trust the coyote time.

> 💡 **Tip:** The Magical Armor cooldown has a visible aura — wait for it to recharge before tanking a hit.
